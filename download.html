Dataset Download

Kilthub

Raw fMRI data is available on Kilthub, Figshare. Please navigate to our dataset page to download our dataset.
Available data include: subject information, image labels, raw fMRI BOLD in dicom format, physiological data, and behavioral data.


New Processed brain responses (as vectors) to images are now available!

OpenNeuro New

Raw (BIDS format), pre-processed fMRI data (BIDS format), ROI masks, scene related activity extracted from regions of interest, and MRIQC reports are now available on OpenNeuro. Please navigate to our dataset page to download our dataset.

Images/Stimuli New

If you would like to download only our images used as stimuli in the study along with provided labels, please download here.
