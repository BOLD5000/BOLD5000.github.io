Dataset Download

Kilthub

Raw fMRI data is available on Kilthub, Figshare. Please navigate to our dataset page to download our dataset.
Available data include: subject information, images used as stimuli, image labels, raw fMRI BOLD in dicom format, physiological data, and behavioral data.
