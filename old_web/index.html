BOLD5000

BOLD5000 is the Brain, Object, Landscape Dataset of 5000 Images.

What is BOLD5000?

BOLD5000 is a large-scale, slow-event fMRI dataset collected on 4 subjects, each observing 5,254 images over 15 scanning sessions. Our images are selected from three computer vision datasets. 1) 1,000 images from Scene Images (250 scene categories, based on SUN categories); 2) 2,000 images from the COCO dataset; and 3) 1,916 images from the ImageNet dataset.
